PROPHET / THE WORLD BECOMES US / 01

Cold open · a measured boundary

THE PLASTIC
WITHIN

Microplastics are tiny plastic fragments shed by everyday materials. They now reach almost every environment on Earth, from ocean depths to Arctic ice, and studies have found them inside the human body, in lung tissue and in artery plaque. Whether they harm health at the population scale is still being measured, and reliable measurement is only now arriving.

OBSERVATION

These particles are now reported across the environment and inside the human body, in lung tissue and in artery plaque removed during surgery. Whether they cause disease across the population has not been established.

Environmental spread Lung detection Plaque detection Causal boundary
DELPHI READ · WHY THIS IS AN EMERGING OPPORTUNITY

The concern is no longer only about polluted oceans. Governments now treat microplastics as a serious human-health question, funding measurement, restricting sources, and standing up dedicated programs, including a $144 million ARPA-H effort to combat microplastics in the body. Delphi's read is that the durable companies form where that response creates real buyers and budgets. Public attention alone does not prove venture returns.

Act 01 · attach the coordinates

A detection needs
a passport.

A single "we found plastic" number means little on its own. It becomes usable only when it travels with its full context, the exposure route, the particle's size and shape, the specific polymer, its added chemistry, the tissue that was sampled and the method that measured it. Strip any one of these and the same result can be read to say almost anything.

Act 02 · define the measurand

Same sample.
Different answer.

The instrument you choose decides which question the sample actually answers. One method counts and identifies individual particles. Another burns the sample to weigh how much polymer is present. Contamination controls, how the sample is prepared and the smallest particle a method can see all shape the final number, so two careful laboratories can report the same sample differently.

Act 03 · keep the categories separate

Every thread stops
where evidence stops.

Evidence comes in levels. Finding a particle in tissue is the first level. Linking it to an outcome, then showing a biological mechanism, then proving cause in an experiment, then proving it in people, each sits a step higher and is harder to earn. Every thread on this page is placed at the highest level the public evidence truly supports, and the step above it is left visibly empty.

Act 05 · connect proof to a buyer

Six layers.
Six different proof gates.

Turning this science into companies runs through six jobs, measure it, map it, attribute cause, prevent release, replace the material and remove what is already in the body. Each job answers to a different buyer on different evidence, so each carries its own proof gate, its own defensibility and its own way of failing. This is the bottleneck map Delphi invests against.
